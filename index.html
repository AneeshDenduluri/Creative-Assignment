About This Website

Purpose

I have decided that for my creative assignment, I will be focusing on researching about 9/11 and how it impacted American Society as we know it. I feel as though my generation as a whole feels disconnected from the tragedy, since we have never lived through that time, and it is extremely easy to feel distanced from the day since it seems so far in the past. I want to place a main focus on the impact the day had on America rather than the actual day itself since I feel like that would be the most beneficial way to learn about the event in a modern setting.

What It Contains

This website has a timeline of the day, stories from survivors and those who were affected by the tragedy, and a page that describes the impact that the day had on America. You may also view a page with my refrences.

References

For this website, I pulled information from the 9/11 Memorial website, the National Commission on Terrorist Attacks upon the United States, the Pew Research Center, and National Archives and Records Administration. These sources supplied me with survivor stories, information about the day, and archived speeches and statements from people like President George W Bush.
