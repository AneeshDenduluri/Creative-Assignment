Timeline of 9/11

5:45

Hijackers Pass Through Security in Maine

Hijackers Mohamed Atta and Abdul Aziz al-Omari board a commuter flight to Boston Logan International Airport, where they connect to American Airlines Flight 11.

7:59

Flight 11 Takes Off

The flight contains 11 crew members (Including Harry Ong's sister Betty Ong), 76 passengers, and 5 hijackers. It is supposed to land in Los Angeles.

8:15

Flight 175 Takes Off

The flight contains 9 crew members, 51 passengers, and 5 hijackers. It is also supposed to land in Los Angeles.

8:19

Betty Ong Contacts Ground Personnel

Betty alerts ground personnel to the situation. She says that hijackers have taken control of the cockpit and hav stabbed a passenger. This man was Daniel M. Lewin who served 4 years in the Israeli Army. It was speculated that he may have tried to stop the hijackers, and is most likely the first victim of 9/11.

8:20

Flight 77 Takes Off

The flight contains 6 crew members, 53 passengers, and 5 hijackers. It also was supposed to land in Los Angeles.

8:24

Flight 11 Hijackers Send a Message

Hijacker Mohamed tried to transmit a message to passengers but accidentally transmitted it to air traffic control. He was trying to calm the passengers by telling them they were going back to the airport.

8:42

Flight 93 Takes Off

The flight contains 7 crew members, 53 passengers, and 4 hijackers. It tok off from Newark Airport and was supposed to land in San Fancisco.

8:46

The North Tower is Attacked

Flight 11 crashes through floors 93-99 of the North Tower. All passengers in the aircraft died instantly, along with hundreds inside the building. The crash trapped hundreds of people above the 91st floor.

8:50

The President is Alerted

President Bush is informed that a plane has hit the North Tower during his visit to Emma E. Booker Elementary School in Florida.

9:00

Flight 175 Contacts Ground Operators

One of the flight attendants informs the ground operators that the plane is being hijacked.

9:03

The South Tower is Attacked

Flight 175 crashes into floors 77-85 of the South Tower. All passengers in the aircraft were killed along with an unknown number of people in the building.

8:50

The President is Alerted

President Bush is informed that a plane has hit the North Tower during his visit to Emma E. Booker Elementary School in Florida.

9:05

The President is Alerted Again

While still in the school, the President is informed that another plane has hit the South Tower. After leaving the school, he makes a minute-long statement that "[t]errorism against our nation will not stand."

9:12

Flight 77 Passengers Contact Family

Flight Attendant Ranee May calls her mom to tell her plane was hijacked. Her mom then alersts American Airlines about the situation. A few minutes later, passenger Barbara Olsen calls her husband and federal officials about the hijacking.

9:37

The Pentagon is Attacked

Flight 77 crashes into the Pentagon. All passengers on board and 125 military personnel in the building were killed.

9:58

Flight 93 calls 911

A total of 37 calls were made by passengers on Flight 93, the last on being by Edward Felt, who contacts an emergency operator about the hijacking.

9:59

The South Tower Collapses

The building burned for 58 minutes before collapsing in 10 seconds. Over 800 civillians and first responders inside and around the building were killed.

10:03

Flight 93 Crashes

The hijackers crash the flight into a field in the Pennsyvania country after a group of passengers and crew storm the cockpit. No one on board survived. The plane was about 20 minutes away from Washington D.C.

10:28

The North Tower collapses

The building burned for 102 minutes before collapsing. Around 1,800 people were killed in the collapse.

17:20

7 World Trade Center Collapses

The building burned for hours. However, no one was in the building or around it, so the collapse resulted in no casualties.

20:30

The President Addresses the Nation

President Bush addresses the nation from the White House about the events that occured that day. Click here to view a summary and anaylsis of his speech.
